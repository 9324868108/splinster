WHY THIS EXISTS

Splinster is a guaranteed delivery platform. These rules are enforced to protect client trust and to keep reliable freelancers rewarded.

Overview

Freelancer Accountability & Penalty System
Purpose: Prevent abuse, protect our guarantee, maintain platform integrity.
Version: 2.0 — Strict Enforcement Model.
⚠️
Important

Splinster is not a freelancing marketplace. We guarantee outcomes. That guarantee only works if every accepted project is treated as a real commitment.

The problem we’re solving

Partial completion abandonment is unacceptable. A freelancer completes part of the work, gets paid, and disappears — leaving Splinster to replace them fast to protect the client. These rules exist to prevent that behavior and to increase trust for clients and reliable freelancers.

Example abuse case
Project: E-commerce Website
Total Value: $1,000
Milestones: 4 × $250 each

FREELANCER THINKS:
"I only want $500. Let me complete 2 milestones,
get paid $500, then abandon the project."

WHAT HAPPENS:
✓ Freelancer gets: $500 for 2 milestones
✓ Platform assigns backup freelancer
✓ Backup gets: $500 for remaining 2 milestones
✓ Platform pays: $1,000 total for $1,000 project

RESULT: Platform breaks even, but...
- Time wasted on replacement
- Client trust at risk
- Platform reputation damaged
- Freelancer gets penalty-free money

This is unacceptable.

Principle

“You signed up to complete projects. Breaking commitment = financial penalty.”

Penalty Structure

If a freelancer abandons a project without a valid emergency, penalties apply to the value of completed work. Repeat abandonment escalates quickly.

Tier Offense Window Penalty Keep Action
Tier 1 — First-time abandonment Rolling 30-day window 15% of completed work value 85% Formal warning issued
Tier 2 — Second abandonment Rolling 30-day window 30% of completed work value 70% Final warning + account flagged (high risk)
Tier 3 — Third abandonment Rolling 30-day window 50% of completed work value 50% 30-day suspension (no new projects)
Tier 4 — Fourth abandonment Rolling 60-day window 100% of completed work value 0% Permanent ban
Tier 1 example
Completed work value: $500
Penalty (15%): $75
Freelancer receives: $425
Platform keeps: $75 (replacement costs)
Tier 2 example
Completed work value: $600
Penalty (30%): $180
Freelancer receives: $420
Platform keeps: $180
Account flagged: High risk
Tier 3 example
Completed work value: $400
Penalty (50%): $200
Freelancer receives: $200
Platform keeps: $200
Suspended: 30 days
Tier 4 example
Completed work value: $700
Penalty (100%): $700
Freelancer receives: $0
Platform keeps: $700 (pays backup freelancer)
Account: PERMANENTLY BANNED

Reasoning: a proven pattern of unreliability.

Penalty reset mechanism

COUNTING WINDOW
- Rolling 30-day window for Tier 1–3
- Rolling 60-day window for Tier 4

RESET CONDITIONS
✓ Complete 5 projects successfully (on-time, quality) → counter resets to 0
✓ 90 days with zero abandonment → counter resets to 0
✗ Any abandonment within 90 days → counter continues

EXAMPLE
Jan 1: Abandonment #1 (15%)
Jan 15: Abandonment #2 (30%)
Feb 1: Complete 5 successful projects → counter resets
Feb 20: Abandonment → treated as first offense (15%)

Valid Emergencies

Valid emergencies receive 0% penalty (freelancer keeps 100% of completed work), if communicated immediately and supported by proof.

Valid (examples)
  • ✓Hospitalization / serious illness (doctor or hospital proof)
  • ✓Immediate family medical emergency (proof required)
  • ✓Death in immediate family (documentation)
  • ✓Natural disaster / force majeure affecting your home (official proof)
  • ✓Region-wide extended outage 5+ days (utility/ISP proof)
  • ✓Device failure (sole device) with reasonable replacement time
Not valid (examples)
  • ✗Got a better paying project elsewhere
  • ✗Changed mind / lost motivation
  • ✗Underestimated complexity
  • ✗Personal vacation without pre-approval
  • ✗“Busy with other work”
  • ✗Client is difficult (escalate to Splinster instead)
How to claim an exception
  1. Notify us immediately (even a 1-line message)
  2. Submit proof within 7 days
  3. We review within 48 hours
  4. If approved: no penalty
  5. If denied: standard penalty applies

Additional Rules

These rules prevent silent failures, improve quality outcomes, and protect delivery timelines.

  1. 1. Communication requirements
    For projects > $5,000:
- Daily status update required
- Miss 2 consecutive days = warning
- Miss 3 consecutive days = red flag (we call you)
- Miss 5 consecutive days = assumed abandonment

For projects < $5,000:
- Update every 2 days minimum

Penalty for non-communication:
1st violation: warning
2nd violation: $50 fine
3rd violation: 10% penalty on current milestone
4th violation: project reassignment + 30% penalty

    Why: silence creates client worry. We cannot guarantee delivery if we don’t know project status.

  2. 2. On-time delivery incentive (bonuses)
    Deliver BEFORE deadline:
- 1–2 days early: 5% bonus
- 3–5 days early: 10% bonus
- 6+ days early: 15% bonus

Example:
Milestone value: $500
Delivered 4 days early
Bonus (10%): $50
You receive: $550

    Why: rewards speed, encourages early delivery, reduces platform risk, and increases client satisfaction.

  3. 3. Quality score system
    Client rates each milestone:
5 stars: bonus 5% on next milestone + priority matching
4 stars: normal payment
3 stars: warning + closer monitoring
2 stars: 5% penalty + quality review
1 star: 15% penalty + mandatory revision + account flagged

Consecutive low ratings:
3 milestones rated 2 stars or below → account suspended for review
  4. 4. Revision limit
    Included revisions: 2 rounds per milestone

Beyond that:
3rd revision: included, but warning
4th revision: $25 fee (deducted from milestone payment)
5+ revisions: case-by-case
- If client scope creep: client is charged
- If poor quality: freelancer bears cost
  5. 5. Project acceptance commitment
    When you click "Accept Project":
You legally commit to:
✓ Complete all milestones
✓ Meet deadlines
✓ Deliver quality work
✓ Communicate proactively

Cooling-off period: 24 hours
- Decline within 24 hours: zero penalty
After 24 hours:
- Abandonment rules apply

Where penalty money goes

30% → Backup Freelancer Incentive
40% → Platform Operations
20% → Client Compensation
10% → Insurance Reserve

Example: $300 penalty
→ $90 backup freelancer bonus
→ $120 operations
→ $60 client compensation
→ $30 reserve fund

Protection mechanisms (fairness)

For freelancers
  • ✓Valid emergency = 0% penalty (with proof)
  • ✓7-day dispute window
  • ✓Performance tiers reward reliability
  • ✓24-hour cooling-off after acceptance
  • ✓Counter reset after sustained success
For platform & clients
  • ✓Penalties fund fast replacement, reducing disruption
  • ✓Deterrence reduces abandonment rates
  • ✓Quality filters improve trust over time
  • ✓Client compensation protects the guarantee reputation

Implementation timeline

Phase 1 (Months 1–3): Soft launch
- Warning only (no financial penalty)
- Track abandonment data
- Educate freelancers

Phase 2 (Months 4–6): Gradual enforcement
- Tier 1: 10% | Tier 2: 20% | Tier 3: 30% | Tier 4: 50%

Phase 3 (Month 7+): Full enforcement
- 15% → 30% → 50% → 100%

Success metrics

1) Abandonment rate target: < 5%
2) Penalty revenue target: ₹1–2 lakh/month
3) Freelancer satisfaction target: 4.5+/5
4) Client trust score target: 95%+
5) Successful replacements target: 100%
6) Appeal success rate target: 10–15%
Summary
  • ✓Prevents abuse (no gaming partial completion)
  • ✓Protects platform (penalties fund replacement)
  • ✓Maintains fairness (valid emergencies exempted)
  • ✓Encourages quality (tiers + bonuses)
  • ✓Builds discipline (commitment has consequences)
  • ✓Strengthens trust (we can guarantee delivery confidently)

Legal Terms

The following clause can be included in the Freelancer Agreement. This is enforced to protect our guarantee.

Clause (copy/paste)
SECTION: PROJECT COMMITMENT & ABANDONMENT POLICY

By accepting a project on Splinster, you commit to completing all milestones as specified.
Abandoning a project without valid emergency will result in the following penalties applied to your completed work value:

1st Abandonment (30-day period): 15% penalty
2nd Abandonment (30-day period): 30% penalty
3rd Abandonment (30-day period): 50% penalty + 30-day suspension
4th Abandonment (60-day period): 100% penalty + permanent ban

VALID EMERGENCIES (with proof, no penalty):
- Medical emergencies (hospital admission, doctor’s certificate)
- Family emergencies (death certificate, disaster proof)
- Force majeure (extended power outage 5+ days, infrastructure failure)

NOT VALID:
- Better paying opportunity elsewhere
- Changed mind
- Underestimated complexity
- Personal vacation without pre-approval

EXEMPTION PERIOD:
You may decline any project within 24 hours of acceptance without penalty.
After 24 hours, commitment is binding.

PENALTY ALLOCATION:
Penalties fund backup freelancer incentives (30%), operational costs (40%), client compensation (20%), and platform reserves (10%).

DISPUTE:
You may dispute any penalty within 7 days by providing evidence.
Final decision by Splinster management within 5 business days.

By clicking "Accept Project," you acknowledge and agree to these terms.

Signature: [Digital acceptance timestamp]
Date: [Project acceptance date]
