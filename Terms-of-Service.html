7. Pricing & Payments (For Freelancers)

Freelancers receive 100% of the agreed rate (Splinster charges 0% commission to Freelancers), subject to the rules below.

Strict accountability (non-negotiable)

Our guarantee depends on execution discipline. We do not entertain abandonment, prolonged silence, or repeated low-quality delivery. By accepting a Project, you commit to completing all milestones and communicating proactively.

Milestone payment timing

Typically within 5 business days after milestone approval (faster tiers may apply).

Completion bonus

A retention/bonus may be released after a short guarantee period (e.g., 30 days), if applicable to your Project.

Abandonment penalties (tiered)

If a Freelancer abandons without a valid emergency, penalties apply to the value of completed work:

  • Tier 1: 15% penalty (first abandonment within 30 days)
  • Tier 2: 30% penalty (second abandonment within 30 days)
  • Tier 3: 50% penalty + 30-day suspension (third abandonment within 30 days)
  • Tier 4: 100% penalty + permanent ban (pattern of abandonment within 60 days)
Example (partial completion abandonment)

Project value: $1,000. Completed work: $500, then abandoned. Tier 1 penalty: 15% of $500 = $75. Freelancer receives: $425. Splinster retains $75 to fund fast replacement.

Valid emergency exceptions (0% penalty) +

With immediate notice and proof within 7 days, we may waive penalties for:

  • Medical hospitalization or serious illness (doctor/hospital proof)
  • Immediate family emergency (appropriate documentation)
  • Force majeure (region-wide outage/disaster with official proof)

Not valid: better-paying project elsewhere, being busy with other work, vacations without pre-approval, loss of motivation.

Cooling-off period

You may decline within 24 hours of accepting a Project with zero penalty. After that, commitment is binding.
