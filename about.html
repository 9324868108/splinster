OUR MISSION

Guarantee Every Project.
Or Refund Every Rupee.

Splinster is not a freelancing marketplace. We’re a delivery guarantee service—work insurance for your project. We vet professionals, monitor execution, run quality checks, and step in when risk appears. If we fail to deliver what we promised, you get 100% money back. This isn’t “better customer support.” It’s a different model: we own the outcome.

How we’re different
  • ✓We vet every professional before they join
  • ✓We monitor projects proactively (not just at the end)
  • ✓If someone fails, we replace immediately—at no extra cost
  • ✓We guarantee on-time delivery—or refund
Business model (the flip)

Traditional platforms charge freelancers ~20% and take zero responsibility. Splinster charges clients a guarantee fee (typically 10–25%) so freelancers keep 0% commission—while we take responsibility for delivery.
