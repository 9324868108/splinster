Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

Terms of Service

At Splinster, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your information. By using our Platform, you consent to the practices described here.

Reminder: Splinster is a guaranteed delivery service / work insurance platform. We use data to help deliver outcomes reliably—including monitoring, intervention, and quality assurance.

Table of contents
1. Information We Collect 2. How We Use Your Information 3. How We Share Your Information 4. Data Security 5. Your Rights & Choices 6. Cookies & Tracking 7. Data Retention 8. International Transfers 9. Children’s Privacy 10. Third-Party Links 11. Changes to this Policy 12. Contact Us

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide
  • Name, email, phone number
  • Business/professional information
  • Project details and communications on the Platform
  • Portfolio/work samples (Freelancers)
  • Government ID or verification info (where required for compliance and fraud prevention)
  • Payment information (processed by third-party payment processors; we may receive limited transaction metadata)
Information we collect automatically
  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage data (pages visited, actions taken, feature usage)
  • Cookies and similar technologies
  • Approximate location (derived from IP)
Information from third parties
  • Social login providers (e.g., Google, LinkedIn, GitHub) if you choose to connect
  • Payment processor confirmations
  • Background check or verification providers (with your consent where required)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Coordinate guaranteed Project delivery (including milestone tracking, QA, and interventions)
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Communicate with you (service messages, updates, notifications)
  • Prevent fraud and maintain Platform security
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze usage and optimize the Platform
  • Send marketing communications (where permitted and/or with consent)

3. How We Share Your Information

Service providers

We may share data with trusted vendors to run the Platform (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting, email/SMS providers, analytics) under confidentiality obligations.

Within the Platform
  • Clients may view Freelancer profile details relevant to delivery (skills, portfolio, experience).
  • Freelancers may view Project details required to deliver work (scope, timelines, requirements).
  • Both parties may see communications necessary to manage milestones and approvals.

We do not share Client payment card details with Freelancers.

Legal requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

Business transfers

If Splinster is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We don’t
  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Publish private messages publicly
  • Store full card details (handled by PCI-compliant processors)

4. Data Security

We protect data with industry-standard security practices, such as HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure infrastructure.

Payment data is handled by PCI-compliant processors. No system is 100% secure; you are responsible for safeguarding your credentials and notifying us of suspected unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)
  • Request data portability

To exercise these rights, email privacy@splinster.com. We typically respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, preferences, analytics, and security.

You can control cookies in your browser settings. Some features may not work properly if you disable essential cookies.

7. Data Retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide services and comply with legal/tax obligations.

  • Project records may be retained for up to 7 years (legal/tax purposes).
  • Backups may retain data for up to 90 days after deletion requests.

8. International Transfers

Splinster operates in India. Your data may be processed in India and, where necessary, other countries through cloud providers and vendors.

We implement appropriate safeguards to protect data across transfers.

9. Children’s Privacy

Splinster is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it.

10. Third-Party Links

Our Platform may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing information.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last Updated” date and may notify you for material changes. Continued use means acceptance.
